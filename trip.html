Honeymoon

🇮🇹 Il nostro viaggio di nozze sarà tra Hawaii e Polinesia Francese 🌺
Vulcani, spiagge nere, snorkeling, immersioni, nuotate con mante, balene e (forse) qualche squalo. Un giro in elicottero sopra i crateri attivi... e poi – per la gioia di Charlotte – tanto tanto sole e relax.

🇬🇧 Our honeymoon will take us from Hawaiʻi to French Polynesia 🌴
Volcanoes, black-sand beaches, snorkeling, diving, swimming with manta rays, whales and (maybe) a few sharks. A helicopter ride over active craters... and then Charlotte’s favourite part: lots of sun, and lots of doing nothing.

🇫🇷 Notre voyage de noces nous emmènera d’Hawaï à la Polynésie française 🌊
